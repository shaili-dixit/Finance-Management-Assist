    Financial literacy 
  
      Financial literacy is the ability to understand and effectively use financial skills, including budgeting, investing, saving, and managing debt. 
   It empowers individuals to make informed decisions about their money, set achievable financial goals, and avoid common pitfalls such as excessive 
   debt or insufficient savings. A strong foundation in financial literacy helps people plan for the future, handle unexpected expenses, and build 
   wealth over time. With the rise of digital banking and complex financial products, financial literacy has become more crucial than ever. By educating 
   themselves on financial principles and practices, individuals can take control of their economic well-being and secure a stable financial future.  
      
      1. **Introduction to Financial Literacy**

           
        - **Objective**:
                To understand the basics of financial literacy and its importance in everyday life.
           
        - **Topics Covered**:

             - What is financial literacy?
             - Why is it important for personal and professional life?
             - Overview of financial concepts (income, expenses, savings, investments, etc.)

        
       2. **Budgeting and Money Management**

        - **Topics Covered**:

             - Tracking income and expenses
